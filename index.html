SARC Yearbook App – Privacy Policy

Last Updated: April 4, 2025
Effective Date: April 4, 2025

1. Introduction

The SARC Yearbook App, developed by the Student Alumni Relations Cell, IIT Bombay, enables the IIT Bombay community to share and preserve memories. This Privacy Policy outlines how we collect, use, and protect your data. By using the App, you agree to these terms. This Privacy Policy aligns with our disclosures in the Google Play Data Safety section.

2. Account Access

Users can sign up using their official email addresses ending with @iitb.ac.in or @iitbombay.org only. Email verification is required via OTP or confirmation link.

3. Prohibition of Copyrighted Content

  • No uploading of copyrighted material (e.g., movie clips, commercial music).
  • Only original content related to IIT Bombay events is permitted.
  • All uploads are manually reviewed within 24 hours.

4. Content Moderation

  • Flagged or filtered content is reviewed before posting.
  • Zero tolerance for harassment, hate speech, or bullying.
  • Violations may result in immediate suspension.

5. Data Collection and Use

We collect:

  • College email address (for signup and identity verification)
  • Uploaded content (photos, videos, text)
  • Device info: type, OS version, and IP address
  • Usage analytics (non-identifiable)

Purpose:

  • Authenticate users and prevent spam
  • Moderate content for guideline compliance
  • Improve performance and fix bugs

We do not collect sensitive data such as financial information, location data, or contact lists.

6. Data Security

  • All data is encrypted in transit (HTTPS) and at rest.
  • Passwords are securely hashed (e.g., bcrypt or Argon2).
  • Role-based access restricts data visibility to authorized personnel.

7. User Reporting

Users can report inappropriate content directly within the app. All reports are reviewed within 24 hours and actioned as necessary.

8. No Ads

The SARC Yearbook App is ad-free and does not display or support advertising of any kind.

9. No Third-Party Services

The App does not use third-party analytics or external service providers. All data is processed internally by the Student Alumni Relations Cell, IIT Bombay.

10. User Rights and Deletion

Users can access or delete their profile and content via the app settings. For permanent deletion, users can email web@sarc-iitb.org. Requests are processed within 7 days.

Users can also disable non-essential notifications and analytics in the app settings.

11. Notifications

The app does not send in-app or push notifications. Users must check manually for updates.

12. Children's Privacy

The App is intended only for users aged 18 and above. Users below 18 are not permitted, in compliance with India's Digital Personal Data Protection Act, 2023.

13. Compliance

  • Android Target: Android 14+
  • Policy available in-app and on the Play Store
  • Compliant with GDPR, CCPA, and India's IT Act and DPDP Act

14. Data Retention

Data is retained until account deletion. Alumni accounts may be archived for internal historical purposes with restricted access.

15. Governing Law

This policy is governed by the laws of India. Any disputes are subject to the jurisdiction of courts in Mumbai, Maharashtra.

16. Contact Us

For any queries, concerns, or deletion requests, contact: web@sarc-iitb.org
