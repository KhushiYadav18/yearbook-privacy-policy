Privacy Policy for SARC Yearbook App

Last Updated: 04-04-2025
Effective Date: 04-04-2025

1. Introduction

The SARC Yearbook App, developed by the Student Alumni Relations Cell, IIT Bombay, enables the IIT Bombay community to share and preserve memories. This Privacy Policy outlines how we collect, use, and protect your data. By using the App, you agree to these terms. This Privacy Policy aligns with our disclosures in the Google Play Data Safety section.

2. Prohibition of Copyrighted Content

  • No copyrighted material may be uploaded (e.g., movies, songs, third-party content).
  • Only original content related to IIT Bombay events is permitted.
  • All content is manually reviewed within 24 hours.

3. Advanced Content Moderation

  • Flagged content is manually approved by an administrator.
  • Harassment, hate speech, or harmful content is not tolerated and may result in suspension.

4. Data Collection & Use

We collect the following:

  • Account Info: College email addresses (@iitb.ac.in or @iitbombay.org)
  • User Content: Uploaded photos, videos, and text
  • Technical Data: Device type, OS version, IP address
  • Usage Analytics: Non-identifiable data to improve app performance

We do not collect sensitive data such as financial information, location data, or contact lists.

5. User Account & Verification

Only users with @iitb.ac.in or @iitbombay.org email addresses may sign up. Accounts are verified via OTP or confirmation link.

6. User Reporting & Transparency

Users can report inappropriate content directly within the app. Reports are reviewed and acted upon within 24 hours.

7. Data Security

  • Data is encrypted in transit (HTTPS) and at rest.
  • Passwords are hashed using bcrypt/Argon2.
  • Role-based access limits admin access to sensitive data.

8. No Ads Policy

The app is 100% ad-free, ensuring a clean and distraction-free experience for users.

9. Third-Party Services

No external analytics or third-party services are used. All data is stored and processed internally by the Student Alumni Relations Cell, IIT Bombay.

10. User Rights

  • Users may access or delete their profile and content within the app settings.
  • To request full account and data deletion, email web@sarc-iitb.org. Requests are processed within 7 days.
  • Users may opt out of non-essential notifications or analytics in app settings.

11. Content Removal & Copyright

  • Content that violates policy is removed within 24 hours.
  • Only original IIT Bombay-related media may be uploaded.
  • Users are responsible for ensuring they have the right to upload shared content.
  • Repeat violations may lead to permanent suspension.

12. Notifications

The app does not send push or in-app notifications. Users are expected to check manually for updates.

13. Compliance

  • Compliant with Android 14+ guidelines
  • Aligns with GDPR, CCPA, and India’s IT Act, 2000
  • Privacy Policy is publicly accessible and available in-app

14. Data Retention

User data is retained until the user deletes their account. Alumni accounts may be archived internally for historical purposes with restricted access.

15. Children’s Privacy

The app is intended only for users aged 18 and above. Users below 18 are not permitted to use the app in compliance with the Digital Personal Data Protection Act, 2023.

16. Policy Updates

Users will be notified of policy updates via email or in-app prompts. Continued use constitutes acceptance of the revised policy.

17. Governing Law

This policy is governed by the laws of India, with any disputes subject to the courts of Mumbai, Maharashtra.

18. Contact Us

For queries, data deletion, or content issues, email us at: web@sarc-iitb.org
